Video and Photography Recordings & Montage

TAM AIR Club is eager to keep learning how to use its drone, GoPro, and camera equipment to capture footage of community events. We create montages to promote your organization/events and leave members/participants and volunteers fun memories.

We're not professionals, but here are a few examples of montages we've been proud to create so far.

Our Equipment

  • DJI Mini 4 Pro drone
  • GoPro cameras
  • Professional photography equipment
  • Video montage production and editing in Adobe Premiere Pro

A PAASS Event - Soccer Fall 2025 - Mill Valley CA

October 2025 - Beautiful morning with the PAASS community in Mill Valley where sports, smiles, and inclusion come together.

Our TAM AIR team was honored to capture the joy and energy of the players through drone, GoPro and photography footage. Big thanks to all the athletes, volunteers, and families who make PAASS so special.

Mill Valley Fall Art Festival 2025

September 2025 - Every year the artists (with diverse specialities) of the community come present their work at the Mill Valley Fall Art Festival.

Bay Area Surf Soccer Training Environment

September 2025 - Created this nice montage of the Bay Area Surf soccer training environment using our DJI Mini 4 Pro drone and a GoPro.
