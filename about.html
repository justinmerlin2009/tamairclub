Our Mission

At TAM AIR, our mission and goal is to conserve nature for a better future by using 3D printed camera equipped drone technology to monitor and protect Marin's natural landscapes from the impacts of wildfires and pollution.

We are a student-led non-profit organization based in Marin County, California. Our team combines passion for technology with environmental stewardship to create innovative solutions for conservation challenges.

Our Goals

  • Develop affordable, 3D-printed drone technology for environmental monitoring
  • Protect Marin County from wildfires through early detection systems
  • Monitor and document pollution in natural habitats
  • Inspire the next generation of environmental scientists and engineers
  • Build community partnerships for conservation efforts

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do
