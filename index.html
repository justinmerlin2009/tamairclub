Our Mission

At TAM AIR, our mission and goal is to conserve nature for a better future by using 3D printed camera equipped drone technology to monitor and protect Marin's natural landscapes from the impacts of wildfires and pollution.

We are a student-led non-profit organization dedicated to combining cutting-edge technology with environmental stewardship. Through STEM education, community outreach, and innovative drone solutions, we aim to make a lasting positive impact on our local environment.
