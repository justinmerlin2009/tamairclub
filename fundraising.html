Help Us Protect Marin

Everyone can help TAM AIR by contributing to our GoFundMe campaign. Your donations directly support our mission to protect Marin County's natural landscapes from wildfires and pollution using innovative drone technology.

But financial contributions aren't the only way to support us. Spreading awareness through word-of-mouth and helping our community stay mindful about environmental protection are equally valuable ways to make a difference.

Where Your Donation Goes

Every contribution helps us advance our mission

Drone Equipment

Funding flight controllers, motors, cameras, and electronic components for our 3D printed UAV fleet.

3D Printing Materials

Purchasing filament, resin, and specialized materials for manufacturing drone components.

STEM Education

Developing educational programs and workshops for local schools and community members.

Research Tools

Supporting our wildfire prediction research with data storage, computing resources, and analysis tools.

Community Events

Organizing beach cleanups, educational workshops, and community outreach programs.

Field Operations

Covering transportation and operational costs for flight testing and environmental monitoring missions.

Other Ways to Help

Not everyone can donate, and that's okay! Here's how you can still make a difference

Spread the Word

Share our mission with friends, family, and your social networks. The more people who know about TAM AIR, the greater our impact can be.

  • Follow us on Instagram and YouTube
  • Share our posts and videos
  • Tell others about our wildfire research
  • Invite friends to our events

Get Involved

Join our team or participate in our community activities. We welcome students, volunteers, and anyone passionate about environmental conservation.

  • Join our club at Tamalpais High School
  • Volunteer for beach cleanup events
  • Attend our educational workshops
  • Connect us with industry professionals
